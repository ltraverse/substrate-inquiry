The Hard Problem

The hard problem of consciousness, approached from many sides.

Twenty thinkers — ten from the past, ten now living — each given a whole page, each on the one insight that is theirs alone. Then The Turn: a working scientist asks why the field can’t settle any of it. And The Glass: one view of what it all might mean.
